About Chris Zemba

Chris Zemba is a seasoned Las Vegas-based guitarist, vocalist, and bandleader delivering a no-nonsense brand of high-energy Blues Rock, R&B, and Soul. Commands the stage with a compelling fusion of contemporary Top 40 rock, pop, R&B, and country hits, seamlessly tailored to any audience.

With over 25 years of professional performance experience, Chris has held residencies at Caesars Entertainment, Stations Casinos, and Boyd Gaming properties across Southern Nevada, and at Mohegan Sun's Wolf Den. As Musical Director of The Rowdy McAren Band — ranked the #1 country rock band in Las Vegas by KWNR Vegas Country Radio — he directed performances for high-profile events including National Finals Rodeo. Since 2011 he has owned and directed Zemba Entertainment, managing multiple acts and partnering with talent agencies to deliver custom entertainment for casino, corporate, and private clients.

Stage Plots & Technical Requirements

Quick reference for production managers and sound crews — full band, solo, and duo/trio configurations. Download the printable PDF.

I. Full Band (4-5 Piece)

  • • Drums upstage center on 6'×8' rug; keys upstage left
  • • Guitar/lead vocal & bass/vocal downstage
  • • 3 vocal positions on boom stands
  • • 4 monitor mixes (Gtr/Voc, Drums, Keys/Voc, Bass/Voc)
  • • 120V power drop at each position

II. Solo Acoustic

  • • Vocal + acoustic guitar via DI box
  • • 1 monitor mix (vocal/guitar blend)
  • • Single 120V power drop
  • • Fully self-contained PA available — zero house production required

III. Duo / Trio

  • • Flexible configuration: guitar + keys, or guitar + bass + drums
  • • Shared input package scaled from the full-band patch
  • • 2-3 monitor mixes
  • • Scales to room size and volume requirements
