The bandleader

About Chris Zemba

Chris Zemba is a seasoned Las Vegas-based guitarist, vocalist, and bandleader delivering a no-nonsense brand of high-energy Blues Rock, R&B, and Soul. He commands the stage with a fusion of contemporary Top 40 rock, pop, R&B, and country, tailored to the audience in front of him.

With over 25 years of professional performance experience, Chris has held residencies at Caesars Entertainment, Stations Casinos, and Boyd Gaming properties across Southern Nevada, and at Mohegan Sun's Wolf Den. Hired as lead guitar player and musical director for The Rowdy Band (Rowdy McAren), he helped lead the band to win the 2012 KWNR Vegas Country Battle of the Bands and a National Finals Rodeo appearance at the Thomas & Mack Center. Since 2011 he has owned and directed Zemba Entertainment, managing multiple acts and partnering with talent agencies to deliver custom entertainment for casino, corporate, and private clients.

Set length

House-friendly

Every input preference is flexible. We work with your house inventory, console, and run-of-show.

Full Band, 4 to 5 piece

  • Drums upstage center on 6' x 8' rug, keys upstage left
  • Guitar/lead vocal and bass/vocal downstage
  • 3 vocal positions on boom stands
  • 4 monitor mixes (Gtr/Voc, Drums, Keys/Voc, Bass/Voc)
  • 120V power drop at each position

Solo

  • Vocal + acoustic guitar via DI
  • 1 monitor mix
  • Single 120V drop
  • Self-contained PA available

Duo / Trio

  • Guitar + keys, or guitar + bass + drums
  • Input package scaled from full band
  • 2 to 3 monitor mixes
  • Scales to room and volume
